Introduce

I am a PhD student in College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University (Hangzhou, China) working with A.P. Kejun Zhang and A.P. Baixi Xing.

I received the B.Eng. degree from Hangzhou Dianzi University, Hangzhou, China, in 2019. My research interests include affective computing, human-computer interaction, and algorithm composing. I recently focus on music generation.

To learn more about my works, please see my projects.
